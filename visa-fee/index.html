Eligibility

  • International Travellers whose sole objective of visiting Kenya is recreation, sight-seeing, casual visit to meet friends or relatives, short duration medical treatment or casual business visit.
  • Passport should have at least 6 months validity from the date of arrival in Kenya. The passport should have at least two blank pages for stamping by the Immigration Officer.

Non-Eligibility

  • Not available for Diplomatic/Official Passport Holders or Laissez-passer travel document holders
  • Not available to people endorsed on Parent's/Spouse's Passport i.e. each person should have a separate passport
  • Not available for International Travel Document Holders
  • Not available for some countries, please check the eligible ones at this link: Check Requirements

Notes:

  • Applicants of the eligible countries/territories may apply online in advance of the date of arrival with a window of 90 days.
  • The validity of eVisa will be 90 days from the date of arrival in Kenya.
  • Single entry is permitted for Kenyan e-Tourist Visa, e-Business Visa and e-Medical Visa.
  • Kenyan Visa online is non-extendable, non-convertible & not valid for visiting protected/restricted and Cantonment Areas.
  • Kenyan eVisa is valid for entry through designated Airports, Seaports and Land Borders.
