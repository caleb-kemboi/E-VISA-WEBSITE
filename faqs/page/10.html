11. What happens if I have lost my passport?

If you lose your passport, you need to register a new passport.

12. I am going on a cruise and need an Kenyan eVisa to enter Kenya, can I apply online?

Yes, you can.

14. Do I need to get an eVisa for my kids?

Yes, everybody must apply for an eVisa regardless of age.

15. My passport expires in less than 6 months, can I apply for Kenyan eVisa?

No, you are not permitted to apply in that case.

16. I have a diplomatic passport, can I apply for Kenyan eVisa?

No, you are not permitted to apply in that case.

19. What activities are permitted with Kenyan e-Tourist Visa?

Kenyan e-Tourist Visa is only entitled to recreation, sightseeing, casual visit (meeting friends or relatives).

20. Who is eligible to apply for Kenyan e-Tourist Visa?

International Traveller who is visiting in Kenya for recreation, sightseeing, casual visit to meet friends and relatives
